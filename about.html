This is WhatDevice

WhatDevice is a web app designed to show information about your computer, tablet, phone, or other internet-connected device. It works on most browsers released over the past decade, using JavaScript and OpenGL. No plugins or external programs are required.

WhatDevice can also share your results via email or download them as a text file. If someone is trying to help you diagnose a technical problem, this is is a great way to let them know exactly what hardware and software you are using.

Finally, WhatDevice is a Progressive Web App, so it can be installed to the home screen on iOS and Android. On newer browsers, WhatDevice can even run when you don't have an internet connection.
