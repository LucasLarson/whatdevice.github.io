About WhatDevice

WhatDevice is a web app designed to show information about your computer, tablet, phone, or other internet-connected device. It does all this through the magic of JavaScript and WebGL - you don't have to download any programs or apps!

WhatDevice supports all modern browsers. Internet Explorer 10 or higher is required.

WhatDevice is still under development, so you may run into bugs.
